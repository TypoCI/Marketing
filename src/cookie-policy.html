Cookie Policy

Last updated: 28th July, 2019

Cookies are small files placed on your hard drive that assist us in providing our services. We use cookies to allow you to enter your password less frequently during a session.

Typo CI aims to use the minimum amount of cookies to provide a functional service, and will not require any third party cookies be enabled to access the service.

Cookie Provider Purpose
_typo_ci_session Typo CI.com Allow user to remain logged in during a session
remember_user_token Typo CI.com Allows user to remain logged in over multiple browser sessions
