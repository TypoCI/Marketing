Typo CI's Subprocessors

Last updated: 3rd January, 2020

This page identifies Subprocessors used by Typo CI to deliver its service, detailing what service is provided and if applicable the location of the Subprocessor.

This page will periodically change as we add or remove Subprocessors, changes that affect the security and/or privacy of the service Typo CI provides will be communicated to customers.

Subprocessor Service Provided Location of Subprocessor
Amazon Web Services Cloud services provider Germany
Heroku Cloud services provider Germany
Sentry Error reporting software United States
Google Cloud services provider United States
SolarWinds Worldwide Application logging services United States
SendGrid Transactional email United States
Zendesk Support services provider United States
